Aggron

Aggron (Japanese: ボスゴドラ Bossgodora) is a dual-type Steel/Rock Pokémon introduced in Generation III.
It evolves from Lairon starting at level 42. It is the final form of Aron.
Aggron can Mega Evolve into Mega Aggron using the Aggronite.

Abilities

Sturdy: The Pokémon will remain with 1 HP if it was going to be knocked out by a move when at full Hit Points, and it is immune to One Hit KO moves.
Rock Head: Does not receive recoil damage from recoil-causing damages.
Heavy Metal:(hidden ability): Doubles the Pokémon's weight.

Weakness

  • Fighting - X4
  • Ground - X2
  • Water - X2
